The Ministry

Jesus Christ Solution Ministry is family of God, dedicated to spreading the good news to all creation, love and live solutions . We believe in the transformative power of faith and believe.

Mission

The mission is to spread the Good news, bring hope to the weary, and uplift souls through the love and teachings/preachings of Jesus Christ.

Founder

Leading with Vision and Faith

Dr. Emily Cheposero Tumwet

With a passion for ministry and a heart for God's people, Dr. Emily Cheposero Tumwet leads Jesus Christ Solution Ministry Church with wisdom, grace, and a commitment to spreading the Good News.

She is a devoted servant of Christ, dedicated to inviting sinners to christ, guiding believers in their spiritual journey and empowering them through the holy spirit. Through teachings, prayers, and biblical insights, she continues to transform lives and bring hope to many.

"Her leadership is centered on faith, love, and the power of the Holy Spirit to bring healing and restoration to Gods creation."
